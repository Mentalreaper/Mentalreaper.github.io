About Me


Hey there, and welcome to my website!

Checkout one of the game images above if you wanna read about that, or just got to ~/projects/ and have a browse.

I enjoy RPGs, Card Games, and making those types of games! I'm usually working on some small project and writing way too much world building for the few characters there!

Currently I'm working on my mini-project: Blade of Roland, a small "souls-like" game (although it's more directly inspired by Sekiro), where Roland is sent by his King (guess which haha) to free—or kill—the King of the Fey, who has seemingly become corrupt. The enemies are based on irish, celtic, and old british folklore. The current king of the fey is thought to be Arthur, but a certain germanic elf-king has taken hold! And, Roland might find that the conspiracy goes even further than this unnamed Elven lord.
